Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

KURAGE AI ("we", "our", "us") operates the KURAGE AI mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Chat Messages: Questions and text you send to the AI assistant. These are used to generate responses and flashcards.
  • Images: Photos you upload for AI analysis. These are processed temporarily and not stored on our servers permanently.
  • Flashcard Data: Cards, decks, and folders you create within the App.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as app opens, feature usage, and session duration.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports to improve app stability.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Browsing history

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered learning assistance
  • To generate and store flashcards
  • To improve the App's features and performance
  • To analyze anonymized usage patterns for product improvement
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions

3. AI-Generated Content

Our App uses Google's Gemini AI to generate responses and flashcard content. Please note:

  • AI-generated content may contain errors or inaccuracies
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy of AI responses
  • Your chat messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
  • Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by their AI services

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Flashcards, decks, and folders are stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Backup: Chat history is backed up to Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication.
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • Access Control: Your data is only accessible with your authenticated account.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Gemini API - AI chat and content generation
  • RevenueCat - Subscription and purchase management
  • Google AdMob - Advertising (Free plan only)

6. Advertising

The Free plan displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad targeting. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. Paid plans (Plus and Ultra) do not display advertisements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. You can also delete your local data by uninstalling the App. Cloud-synced data will be deleted upon request within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date.
